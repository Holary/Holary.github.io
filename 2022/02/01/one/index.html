GAMES
PEOPLE PLAYS

Dr Eric Berne,like his father,graduated from the Faculty of Medicine,McGill university,Montreal,Canada,and later moved to the United states，Where he had his internship in psychiatry at the New Haen Hospital and the Yale Institate of Human Relations.

埃里克·伯恩博士，像他的父亲一样，毕业于加拿大蒙特利尔麦吉尔大学医学院，后来移居美国,他在纽黑文医院和耶鲁大学人类关系学院进行精神病学实习。

He also studied at the New York and san Francisco Psychoanalytic Institutes.

他还在纽约和旧金山精神分析研究所学习。

During the Second World War he served in the U.S.

第二次世界大战期间，他在美国服役。

Army Medical Corps and was discharged with the rank of major.

陆军医疗队，并以少校军衔退伍。

After wards he became aconsultant in psychiatry and neurology to the Surgeon General of the U.S,Army Surgeon General.

后来他成为了美国陆军卫生局局长的精神病学和神经病学顾问。

He was a practising psychiatrist in Carmal and San Francisco,California,a lecturer at the University of California Medical School,and Consultant in Group Therapy at McAuley Clinic in San Francisco.

他曾是加利福尼亚州卡玛尔和旧金山的职业精神医生、加利福尼亚大学医学院的讲师和旧金山麦卡利诊所的团体治疗顾问。

He was a corresponding member of the Indian Psychiatric Society,chairman of the board of trustees of the Innational Transactional Analysis Association and editor of the Transactional Analysis Bulletin.

他是印度精神病学协会的通讯成员，国际交易分析协会的董事会主席及《交易分析公报》的编辑。

He also wrote The Mind in Action,Transactional Analysis in Psychotherapy,The Structure and Dynamics of Organizations and Groups and Principles of Group Treatment.

他还着手《行动中的思想》、《心理治疗中的交易分析》、《组织额团体的结构与动力》和《团体治疗的原则》。

A Layman’s Guide to Psychiatry and psychoanalysis and sex in Human loving are also available in Penguins.

关于精神病学和精神分析以及人类爱中的性的门外汉指南也可以企鹅身上找到。

While at college he wrote fore the Canadian magazine Forum,the London Adelphi and other periodicals.

上大学时他为加拿大杂志《论坛》、《伦敦阿德尔菲》和其他期刊撰稿。

He died in 1970.

他死于1970.

ERIC BERNE
埃里克·伯思
