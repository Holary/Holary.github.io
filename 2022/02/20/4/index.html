SEMANTICS

For conciseness,the games are described primarily from the made point of view unless they are clearly feminine.

游戏主要是男性的角度来描述的，除非它们明显是女性化的。

Thus the chief player is usually designated as ‘he’,but without prejudice,since the same situation,unless otherwise indiated could as easily be outlined with ‘she’,mutatis mutandis.

因此，主玩家通常被称为“他”，但没有偏见，因为同样的情况，除非另有说明，可以很容易地用“她”来概括，作必要的修改。

If the woman’s role differs signifiantly from the man’s,it is treated separat.

如果女性的角色与男性的角色明显不同，则会被分开对待。

The vocablar and viewpoint are priarily oriented toward the practising clinician but members of other professions may find this book interesting or useful.

书中的词汇和观点主要面向临床医生，但其他职业的人可能会觉得这本书有趣或有用。

Transactional game analysis should be clearly distinguished from its growing sister science of mathematical game analysis,although a faw of the terms used in the text,such as ‘payoff’,are now respectably mathemat.

事物博弈分析应该与其不断增长的数学博弈分析姊妹科学有明显的区别，虽然文本中使用的术语，例如“收益”，现在具有可观的数学意义。

For a detailed review of the mathematical theory of games see Games & Decisions,by R.D.Luce and H.Raiffa2.

对于一个对博弈数学理论的详细回顾参考《游戏与决策》，R.D.Luce和H.Raiffa。

Carmel,California,May 1962.

1962年五月份加利福利亚卡梅尔。
